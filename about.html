ABOUT THE COMIC COVE

Your local comic shop with a passion for stories and community.

Our Mission

"To bring fans together and provide the best comics, collectibles and a place where fans belong."

Our Story

The Comic Cove started with a love for comics and a dream to build a place where fans belong. Founded in Gaborone, Botswana, we set out to bring the world of comics to our community — from Marvel blockbusters to indie gems, manga series and rare collectibles.

Over the years we have grown from a small shelf of comics to a full-scale shop and community hub. We host events, connect creators with fans and champion storytelling in all its colourful, action-packed, emotional glory.

Whether you are a lifelong collector or opening your very first comic, you are welcome here. The Comic Cove is more than a shop — it is a home for the community.

What We Stand For

Community First

Every decision we make is shaped by our community. Fans, creators and collectors all belong here.

Quality Curation

Every title in our catalogue is hand-picked. We stock what fans actually want to read and collect.

Passion-Driven

We are fans ourselves. Our enthusiasm for comics is genuine and it shows in everything we do.

Local & Global

Rooted in Gaborone, connected to the world. We source titles globally while serving our community locally.
